## What is a Creator Card?

The Creator Card is one of the core utilities within the Accio ecosystem dashboard. It is a dynamic, shareable visual asset designed specifically for Access Protocol creators to showcase their on-chain success and community growth in a sleek, customizable format.

## Utility for the ACS Community

For creators, building and maintaining a community is everything. The Creator Card serves as a verified, visual resumé of a creator's success within the ACS ecosystem. 

- **Marketing Asset:** Creators can share these cards on social media to attract new subscribers, demonstrating traction and community trust.
- **Transparency:** By displaying real-time on-chain data (like Total ACS Locked), creators provide a transparent view of their network.
- **Milestone Celebration:** Easily generate updated cards to celebrate hitting new subscriber milestones or TVL (Total Value Locked) targets.

## Data Displayed

Accio queries the Solana blockchain to extract the following information for Creator Cards:

| Metric | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Creator Name** | The registered name of the creator's pool. |
| **Total ACS Locked** | The aggregate amount of ACS staked by all subscribers in this specific pool. |
| **Total Stakers** | The total number of unique wallets currently supporting the creator. |
| **Rank** | The creator's global standing within the Access Protocol ecosystem (e.g., #14). |

## Generation Workflow

### 1. Copy Your Pool Address
First, go to your creator page on the [Access Hub](https://hub.accessprotocol.co/) and copy your unique pool address as shown below.

  <img src="/images/placeholders/creatormode1.png" alt="Accio Hub Copy Address UI" />
</Frame>

### 2. Open Accio & Select Creator Mode
Navigate to our live application at [accio-v1.vercel.app](https://accio-v1.vercel.app/) and select **Creator** under the Mode Selector.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholders/cr2.png" alt="Accio Creator Mode UI" />
</Frame>

### 3. Paste Your Pool Address
Paste the pool address you copied from the Access Hub into the input field under the Mode Selector.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholders/cr3.png" alt="Paste Pool Address UI" />
</Frame>

### 4. Fetch Details
Click the **Fetch Pool Stats** button. Accio will instantly query the Solana blockchain and generate your dynamic card based on your real-time on-chain data.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholders/cr4.png" alt="Accio Fetch Pool Stats UI" />
</Frame>

### 5. Customize and Export
Use the visual customizer in the sidebar to adjust themes, colors, and background art. 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholders/creatormode3.png" alt="Accio Edit Options UI" />
</Frame>

Once you are satisfied with the look, click **Export** to download a high-resolution image of your card.
