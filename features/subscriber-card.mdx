## What is a Subscriber Card?

The Subscriber Proof-of-Support card is a specialized visual utility within the Accio suite that verifies a user's commitment to a specific Access Protocol creator. It acts as an on-chain receipt, turned into a visually striking badge of honor.

## Utility for the ACS Community

Access Protocol thrives on the relationship between creators and their most dedicated fans. The Subscriber Card gamifies and socializes this relationship.

- **Verified Flexing:** Subscribers can prove their early or substantial support for a creator in Discord or on X. It is a verifiable "I was here" statement.
- **Community Building:** Helps creators easily identify and recognize super-fans within their community.
- **Organic Marketing:** When subscribers share their Proof-of-Support cards, they organically promote both the creator and the Access Protocol ecosystem.

## Data Displayed

To ensure privacy while proving support, the Subscriber Card displays specific, wallet-related metrics:

| Metric | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Wallet Address** | A truncated version of your public key to verify ownership (e.g., `8k...9A`). |
| **Total ACS Locked** | The total aggregate amount of ACS you have staked across the platform. |
| **Staked APY** | The current Annual Percentage Yield (APY) representing your earning rate. |
| **Active Pool** | The number of Access Protocol creator pools you are currently subscribed to. |

## Generation Workflow

### 1. Select Staker Mode
Navigate to the Accio dashboard and select **Staker** under the Mode Selector.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholders/sub1.png" alt="Select Staker Mode UI" />
</Frame>

### 2. Paste Wallet Address
Copy the **public Solana wallet address** that you use to subscribe to creators on Access Protocol, and paste it into the input field.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholders/sub2.png" alt="Paste Wallet Address UI" />
</Frame>

### 3. Fetch Data
Click **Fetch Pool Stats** and wait for Accio to query your on-chain subscription data to generate your base card.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholders/sub3.png" alt="Fetch Data UI" />
</Frame>

### 4. Customize and Export
Accio will automatically build your card. Use the side panel to customize your background options and layout.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholders/sub4.png" alt="Accio Staker Customizer UI" />
</Frame>

Once it looks perfect, simply click **Export** to save a high-resolution version of your image.

### 5. Final Result
Here is your final Proof-of-Support card, ready to be shared with your favorite creator's community!

  <img src="/images/placeholders/subcard.jpg" alt="Accio Subscriber Final Card Result" />
</Frame>

<Info>
  **No Connection Required:** Accio operates solely via public addresses. You do not need to connect your wallet, ensuring a perfectly safe and frictionless experience.
